    Hello there! My name is Hiram and welcome to my addiction recovery journal.

    I don't know about you, but I'm tired of wasting so many hours looking at things I shouldn't be at the computer. Join me as I explore the depths of how I got to that point, what I can do about it, and finally how to achieve lasting freedom.

    How it all began

    I remember the night like it was yesterday.

    When the love of my life, the one who I was going to build a life and family with, slammed the door on me. Deep down I knew why. This was going to be the last time, I promise!

    And yet, for reasons I couldn't fathom, I did it again...

    There I was, in the aftermath of yet another relapse when she walked in, took one look at the devastation, and simply began packing her bags. I didn't try and stop her.

    Today, I do my utmost to honour her memory, and to mourn the life we could've had together.

    How I stay committed to the path

    The first way I remain on the path is described above, because if I want anything it's not to go through that again! Should I be fortunate to find another sweetheart, the last thing I want to do is drive her away.

    Secondly, I want to use this journal as a way of uncovering unconscious patterns that regularly escape my attention. I want to master my mind, so that it doesn't master me.

    Finally, I think of all the people and their potential trapped by the same demons I'm facing. The best way to learn something is to teach it!
